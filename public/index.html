I thrive when facing unexpected challenges and venturing into uncharted territory. My engineering background combined with product management expertise allows me to bridge technical possibilities with business outcomes.

My approach centers on creating new capabilities rather than just optimizing existing ones. I craft POCs to reduce risks and sell ideas, break down complex problems into smaller milestones, and build bridges between stakeholders to drive execution from inception to delivery.

With experience across 7 European markets and versatility from ideation to maintenance, I've consistently delivered results that matter:
